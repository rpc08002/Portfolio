About Me

Hey there and welcome to my portfolio! I am a full stack web developer who has always had a fascination for how things work and WHY they work that way. While the year 2020 was dark and gloomy, I took the extra time at home and enrolled in UConn's Coding Bootcamp (best Instagram targeted ad I have clicked on!).

Before I dove into coding, I earned 7 years of working as a sales professional, 5 of those years managing and building a team in the automotive and financial industries. My years at Enterprise Rent-A-Car taught me how to run an actual business and grow it. I met amazing people at this company and couldn't be happier my career started here for the foundation it gave me in becoming a successful business person.

At TouchSuite, I gained experience dealing with business to business sales by providing payment processing solutions to small-medium sized businesses. It was a pleasure to help business owners grow their own business with our payment solutions to improve cash flow and customer loyalty.

After spending four years in the sunshine state I moved back to Stamford, CT, and landed a growth sales job at Indeed.com with a focus on the automotive industry. I am now helping people get jobs and business owners reach their short and long term hiring goals. My success with sales the past 7 years will translate well into coding as I am eager to continue learning and developing my skills. I am open to junior web developer roles, volunteer work, part-time/contract positions, anything to continue building my skillset. I've accumulated experience with Web Dev over the course of several full stack builds using the SERN stack: SQL, Express, React.js, and Node.js
